how to play:

press the start button!

there can be only two players

each player starts with $1,000

at each turn, press the agree button to continue

each round is composed of two (2) turns, and after the round is finished, every player will receive $50

click on the next button after each player finishes their turn to continue onto the next round

once your balance goes to 0 (or below), you automatically lose and the other player wins.

the goal is to make as much money as possible!
